Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

At Unfuck Your Web, we're serious about fixing broken websites. We're also serious about protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Name: So we know what to call you
  • Email address: So we can actually respond
  • Website URL: So we can see what we're working with
  • Problem description: So we know what's fucked
  • Selected issues: The items you click on our diagnostic checklist

Automatically Collected Information

Like every other website on the internet, we collect:

  • IP address: For security and analytics
  • Browser and device information: To make sure our site works on yours
  • Page views and interactions: To understand what people actually do on our site
  • Referral source: How you found us (Google, ads, word of mouth, etc.)
  • UTM parameters: Marketing campaign tracking codes in the URL

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry: The whole point of the form
  • Evaluate your website: So we can give you an accurate assessment
  • Send you a proposal: With pricing and timeline
  • Provide our services: If you hire us to unfuck your web presence
  • Improve our marketing: Understanding what messages and channels work
  • Communicate about our services: Relevant updates, not spam

3. Cookies and Tracking

Meta Pixel

We use the Meta Pixel (formerly Facebook Pixel) to:

  • Track conversions from our advertising campaigns
  • Build audiences for retargeting ads
  • Optimize ad delivery to people most likely to need our services

The Meta Pixel collects information about your visit and may share it with Meta (Facebook). You can opt out of personalized ads in your Facebook settings.

Session Storage

We use browser session storage to temporarily save UTM parameters and referral information during your visit. This data is cleared when you close your browser.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information with:

Service Providers

  • Cloudflare: Hosting, security, and content delivery
  • Meta (Facebook): Advertising and analytics via Meta Pixel
  • Email service: To send you responses and communications

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process.

Business Transfers

If we sell or merge our business, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

We Do NOT:

  • Spam you with unrelated offers

We protect your information using industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit

  • Regular security updates

No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your data.

To exercise these rights, email us at: privacy@unfuckyourweb.com
